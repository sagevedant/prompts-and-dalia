3 Red Flags That Kill Brands
Red Flag 01
They Won't Let You Visit Unannounced
Any manufacturer requiring 48h+ notice before a facility visit is hiding something ‚Äî hygiene lapses, unlicensed machinery, shared allergen lines. Rule: always do one surprise visit. If refused, walk away immediately.
Red Flag 02
They Quote Before Seeing Your Recipe
A serious manufacturer quotes after understanding your moisture content, spice volumes, and packaging spec. If they give you a per-unit rate in the first 2 minutes ‚Äî they're guessing. That guess gets revised upward after you've committed.
Red Flag 03
No Traceability on Raw Material Sourcing
If a CM can't show invoices or certificates for their suppliers ‚Äî you own the liability if a contamination complaint surfaces. In India's food market, adulteration lawsuits hit brands, not manufacturers.
