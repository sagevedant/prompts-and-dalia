Colour Palette With Emotional Rationale
Terracotta
#C4622D
Clay pots. Turmeric-adjacent warmth. The exact opposite of the clinical green your customer is tired of.
Forest Green
#2C4A35
Says "herb garden", not "hospital". Natural without the clinical connotation of lime green.
Linen Off-White
#F5F0E8
Warm white = handmade, analog, human. Pure white = clinical, sterile, pharmaceutical.
Wheat Gold
#D4A96A
Used sparingly. Like a spice — a little is aromatic. Too much is overwhelming.
What Annam Looks Like
Primary Pack — Stand-Up Kraft Pouch
140mm × 230mm × 80mm gusset. Matte kraft laminate. No gloss anywhere. The matte finish carries 70% of the premium perception.
Mailer Box — D2C Delivery Experience
E-flute corrugated kraft. Custom terracotta tape with repeating "Annam" text. "Good morning." inside the flap. Highest ROI element at ₹1.50 per unit.

The Hidden Element · ₹0 Extra
Hidden Inside The Zip-Lock · Visible Only On First Open
Hidden. Discovered. Photographed.

A hand-lettered Sanskrit verse from the Annapurna Stotram printed inside the top gusset of the pouch — visible only when you open the zip-lock for the first time. It's just print on an inside flap that would otherwise be blank. It costs ₹0 extra. First-time unboxers photograph this. Every time.
